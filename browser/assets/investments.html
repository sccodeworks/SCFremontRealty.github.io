Investor Requirements

Accredited Investor Status

You must meet one of the following SEC criteria:

  • Individual income exceeding $200,000 (or $300,000 joint) in each of the past two years
  • Net worth exceeding $1,000,000 (excluding primary residence)
  • Professional certifications (Series 7, 65, 82)
  • Entity with assets exceeding $5,000,000

Investment Minimums

Minimum investment amounts vary by opportunity:

  • NNN Retail: $50,000
  • Industrial: $75,000
  • Multifamily: $50,000

Lower minimums may be available for existing investors or through self-directed IRAs.
